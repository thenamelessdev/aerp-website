Bot commands

You can find the AER bot's commands


Fun commands:

/astronomy-pic - gets you the astronomy pictue of the day with NASA api
/blackmagic - gives you information about a given Roblox user ID
/feetpics - gives you some feetpics


Rank commands:

/rank - shows you your level
/level leaderboard - shows you the level leaderboard
/invites - shows you how many people you have invited
/invites-leaderboard - shows you the invites leaderboard


Other commands:

/report - reports a member
